Joker Is a SuperVillan created by Bill Finger, Bob Kane, and Jerry Robinson. Joker first apperared in the comic book Batman in April 25, 1940. In his first apperance he was introduced as a psychopath with a sense of humor. 6 people have played the Joker role these people are Cesar Romero, Jack Nicholson, Mark Hamill, Heath Ledger, Jared Leto, and Joaquin Phoneix. Joker fell into a tank of chemical waste which bleaches his skin white and turns his hair green and lips bright red. The Joker doesn't have any Superhuman ablilites, Instead he uses his expertise in chemical engineering to develop poisonous or lethal concoctions, and thematic weaponry, including razor-tipped playing cards, deadly joy buzzers, and acid-spraying lapel flowers.
